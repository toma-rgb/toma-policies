Data Deletion Instructions

If you want us to delete data associated with your WhatsApp messages:

  1. Send an email to adamiatoma@gmail.com from the email you control.
  2. Include your WhatsApp number (in international format) and the phrase “Data Deletion Request.”
  3. We will confirm and delete conversation state and related logs within a reasonable timeframe.

You can also trigger an in-chat reset by sending “reset” (if implemented), which clears your session state.
